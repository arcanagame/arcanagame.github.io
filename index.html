Arcana will be a massively multiplayer online game with cool animals and community-centered gameplay.


Welcome to the homepage of the Arcana Project, a non-profit group that aims to make a cool game for enthusiasts of MMOs, animals, and collaborative works. Our members are from all over the world, and have hobbyist experience across many different fields.
Our goal is to create an awesome game that anyone of any age can enjoy genuinely. We also want to create a community environment with our game that allows people of all demographics to contribute in both big and small ways.
